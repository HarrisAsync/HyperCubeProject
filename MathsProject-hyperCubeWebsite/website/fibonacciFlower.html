Fibonacci Flower

The Fibonacci sequence is a pattern formed by the addition of the previous two numbers in the sucession (beginning with 0 and 1). It is found throughout the natural world and the spiral pattern on sunflower seeds is no exception. If the spirals are counted in a consistant manner from the centre outwards a fibonacci sequence will always emerge. 137.5 degrees is the golden angle of the spiral in which the Fibonacci flower is produced.
