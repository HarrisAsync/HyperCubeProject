A gallery of various interactive visualisations that demonstrate the nature and beauty of mathematics.

The Die Hard Problem

Quickly solving the water jug problem using this trick may save you from Dying Hard. Watch this laser bounce around a parallelogram to find a solution to your jug problem.

Difficulty: Moderate
The Fibonacci Flower

If you look closely, you can see the mathematics of nature such as finding the curious Fibonacci sequence on flowers and other patterns.

Difficulty: Easy
Penrose Tiling

This 2-shape aperiodic tiling does not produce the same tiling by shifting these shapes by any finite distance, without rotation. See for yourself.

Difficulty: Moderate
The N-cube

Understand the dimensionality of various N-cubes, rotate them about different axes to view their underlying structure. See for yourself!

Difficulty: Hard
