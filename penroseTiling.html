Penrose Tiling

Named after the mathematician and physicist Roger Penrose, this tiling is aperiodic, meaning that the tiles on the plane do not contain arbitrarily large repeating patches. The tiling is typically formed with only 2 shapes, a kite and dart. For more information check out this Veritasium video.
